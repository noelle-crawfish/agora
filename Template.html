About

Who are we?

This web application was designed by Noelle Crawford, Marley Magee, Claire Pare, and Ashley Tussanee at SM Hacks II.

We are all high school students in California. In our spare time we love to code!

What is Agora?

As students, we found that we wanted an outlet to ask for help on homework and communicate with fellow students and teachers. We came up with the idea of Agora to provide a fun place for students to get help, chat with others, coordinate school activities, and so much more!

Enjoy!

According to Oxford Dictionary...

Agora: noun, (in ancient Greece) a public open space used for assemblies and markets.
